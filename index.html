Delete Account - ReelSound

Users can delete their account anytime inside the ReelSound app.

How to Delete:

  1. Open ReelSound app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

What Happens:

  • Your profile will be permanently deleted
  • Your saved data will be erased
